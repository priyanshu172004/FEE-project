Automatic Transcript Extraction

The app automatically extracts the transcript from YouTube videos, eliminating the need for manual input. Users simply paste the video URL, and the app fetches the entire transcript for processing.

Summarization Options

Users can choose different summarization modes (e.g., concise, detailed, or bullet points). This flexibility allows the summary to be tailored to specific needs, such as a quick overview or an in-depth review.

Keyword Highlighting

The app highlights key terms and phrases in the summary, making it easy to identify the most important points. This feature is particularly useful for quickly scanning the summary for relevant information.

Language Support

The app supports multiple languages, both for transcript extraction and summarization. This feature is ideal for users who work with content in different languages or need summaries in specific languages.

Downloadable Summaries

Users can download the summarized text in various formats (e.g., PDF, Word, or plain text). This ensures easy sharing and offline access to the summarized content.

Time-Stamped Summaries

The app provides summaries with time stamps, allowing users to jump directly to specific parts of the video. This is especially useful for longer videos where only certain sections are relevant.
